Our Commitment to Advanced Technology

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we are committed to staying at the forefront of dental technology to provide our patients with the best possible care and most comfortable experience.

1. Digital X-Rays

Our state-of-the-art digital X-ray system provides high-resolution images with significantly less radiation exposure compared to traditional X-rays. This technology allows for quicker and more accurate diagnoses, ensuring that we can address dental issues promptly and effectively.

  • 90% less radiation than film X-rays
  • Instant digital images for faster treatment
  • Enhanced diagnostic accuracy

2. Intraoral Cameras

Our intraoral cameras offer a detailed view of your mouth, allowing us to capture high-quality images of your teeth and gums. This technology enhances our ability to detect and diagnose dental problems early, and it also helps us to better explain treatment options to our patients.

  • High-definition magnification up to 40x
  • Real-time imaging during examinations
  • Patient education through visual explanations

3. Laser Dentistry

We have incorporated advanced laser technology into our practice, which allows for minimally invasive procedures with reduced pain and faster healing times. Laser dentistry is used for a variety of treatments, providing precision and comfort.

  • Gum disease therapy without cutting
  • Cavity detection and preparation
  • Professional teeth whitening
  • Reduced need for anesthesia

4. CAD/CAM Technology

Our CAD/CAM (Computer-Aided Design and Computer-Aided Manufacturing) system enables us to create precise and custom dental restorations such as crowns, bridges, and veneers, in a single visit. This technology ensures a perfect fit and natural appearance, providing our patients with durable and aesthetically pleasing results.

  • Same-day restorations
  • Digital precision scanning
  • Natural-looking ceramic materials
  • Eliminate temporary restorations

5. 3D Printing

We utilize 3D printing technology to create accurate dental models, surgical guides, and custom appliances. This innovation allows for more precise treatment planning and improved outcomes for our patients, revolutionizing how we approach complex cases.

  • Custom surgical guides for implants
  • Precise orthodontic appliances
  • Patient-specific night guards
  • Rapid prototyping for complex cases

6. Teledentistry

Our teledentistry services provide convenient access to dental care from the comfort of your home. Through virtual consultations, we can assess your dental concerns, provide professional advice, and develop treatment plans without the need for an in-office visit – perfect for follow-ups or initial assessments.

  • Secure video consultations
  • Remote oral health assessments
  • Prescription management
  • Follow-up appointment planning
