About Us

Located at 33 Fantail Street in Razole, we've been a cornerstone of the community for over three decades. With more than 50 years of combined experience, our team offers friendly, reliable, and compassionate dental care for all ages – from kids' first visits to senior restorative treatments.

We understand every patient's unique needs, providing a full range of services including preventive check-ups, cleanings, implants, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, root canals, and emergencies. Patient education is key, with guidance on nutrition, hygiene, and informed decisions.
